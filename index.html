Welcome to jSynth: a digital synthesizer built with Javascript!

To get started playing, use your computer keyboard. The home key row corresponds to all of the white keys on a piano, and the row above corresponds to the black keys.
For a more in depth description of what everything does, hover your mouse over the located on each component.
